Here’s a practical guide to Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) from a team* that has spent the last six months running dozens of research studies to figure it out:

*the Ahrefs team

▪️Start by measuring your current AI visibility. Check how often your brand appears in the AI chatbots you care about, and compare that to competitors. There are many “AI visibility tracker” tools out there, including ours.

▪️Find which domains and pages in your niche are most-cited by AI. In Ahrefs’ Brand Radar, put your keyword in the “Market or niche” field and leave everything else blank - this will show the top-cited domains and pages in that niche.

▪️Equip your PR, partnerships, and influencer teams with the above list. Set goals to boost your brand’s presence on these sites and pages. But don’t stop there — your AI visibility is shaped by mentions on all sorts of authoritative websites across the web. Use that initial list as a starting point and seek out more relevant sites to get featured on.

▪️Make sure your own site tells your story. AI can’t read your Slack, Basecamp, or email threads. If you want AI (and people) to know something about your business, make it clear and accessible on your website.

▪️Find your own most-cited, highest AI-traffic pages, and make sure they're accurate, up-to-date, and well structured.

▪️Write clearly. Use simple, declarative sentences. Avoid confusing AI with rambling, unfocused content.

▪️Use UGC-heavy platforms like Reddit and Quora, which AI likes to cite. Join relevant communities, engage in relevant conversations, and never spam.

▪️ Repurpose your content into short videos. Embed them in articles, share them on social media, and upload to YouTube (heavily cited by LLMs).

▪️ Publish the kind of content LLMs love: statistics, experiments, product comparisons, and original research.

▪️ Identify topic gaps where your competitors are mentioned but not you (Ahrefs Brand Radar makes this easy). Create content, on-site and off-site, to close these gaps.

▪️ Bet early on emerging topics. Map clusters of related questions around a given topic. Use PAA and SERP data to mock up what "query fan-out" might look like.

▪️ Create branded search demand. Publish original research and coin unique frameworks. Then promote your content hard to drive awareness. This way people will start searching for your brand - “ahrefs study on X,” “ahrefs framework on Y.”

▪️Catch URLs which were hallucinated by AI and redirect them to relevant resources. (Ahrefs’ free Web Analytics tool will soon filter these automatically.)

AEO isn’t SEO.

SEO focuses on optimizing your own site to rank in search. AEO is about getting your brand mentioned by AI — which largely depends on how you’re represented across the entire web.
