I once sat down and analyzed what was really happening with AI search.
Not because I was curious - but because I realized everyone was targeting keywords... and getting terrible results.
So I stopped focusing on single keywords. Stopped old-school SEO thinking. Stopped guessing what works.
Jakub Sadowski research showed me what most marketers miss about AI search.
They build strategies based on outdated thinking.
Since then, I've worked with the AI - not against it. Fewer keywords. More themes. Focus on what really matters.
AI generates 2-5 extra queries per search. Only 0.6% show up consistently. But 84% overlap with original SERP.
If your SEO can't adapt to AI... it's not strategy. It's wishful thinking.
