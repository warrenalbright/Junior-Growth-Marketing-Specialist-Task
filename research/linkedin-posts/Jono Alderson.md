AI is about to make every gap between your marketing and reality easier to find.

For years, companies benefited from the fact that scrutiny was expensive. 

Nobody had time to compare every claim against every review, complaint, policy, pricing page and awkward contradiction, so we relied on shortcuts: brands, rankings, awards, testimonials, and the general theatre of competence.
That worked when people had to do the due diligence themselves.

Now their AI can do it for them.

I wrote about why cheap scrutiny changes everything, and why visibility is becoming less of a prize, and more of a threat.
