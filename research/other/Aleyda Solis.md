## Article 1: Key Traditional vs AI Search Differences - A Visual Comparison
- **URL:** https://www.aleydasolis.com/en/ai-search/key-traditional-search-ai-comparison/
- **Date Published:** July 26, 2025
- **Type:** Blog Article with Visual Infographic

### Summary
Aleyda breaks down the 5 key differences between traditional and AI search
that most impact search marketers:

1. **Search Behavior** — Short keyword queries vs long conversational multi-turn queries
2. **Query Handling** — Single query match vs Query Fan-Out / multiple sub-queries
3. **Optimization Target** — Page level relevance vs Passage/Chunk level relevance
4. **Authority & Popularity Signals** — Links/engagement vs Mentions/Citations & Entity-based authority
5. **Results Presentation** — Ranked list of pages vs Single synthesized answer with citations
