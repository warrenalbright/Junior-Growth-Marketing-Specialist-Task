## Article 1: AI Search Study - Translating Content Drives 5.33X Visibility Increase
- **URL:** https://www.spicymargarita.co/blog/ai-search-study-translating-content-drives-ai-visibility
- **Type:** Original Research Study (B2B SaaS Client Data)
- **Author:** Ben Goodey
- **Partner:** Weglot

### Summary
Ben ran a controlled experiment with a B2B SaaS client (DR 62, ~5K monthly
traffic): translated 14 existing blog posts into Spanish and German via
Weglot, then measured the impact on traditional search and AI search
visibility over 60 days. No rewrites or edits — purely automated translation.

### Key Findings
- **Google Search (first 28 days):** 102 clicks and 27,797 impressions
  from brand new translated pages. Product listicles drove 78 clicks,
  educational guides drove 24.
- **Bing AI Citations (first 30 days):** 1,100 total AI citations across
  both markets — Spanish (767) and German (333).
- **AI Visibility in LLMs (Peec AI measurement):**
  - Spanish: baseline ~6% → 32% by month 2 (5.33x increase)
  - German: baseline ~4.5% → 16.8% in month 1
- **Revenue impact:** Correlated with +3 Spanish, +1 Mexican, +2 German
  qualified demo requests in April vs previous months.

### Why This Matters for AI-Powered SEO Content Production
This is one of the few published studies showing a direct causal link
between content actions (translation) and measurable AI visibility uplift
across multiple LLMs. Directly applicable to B2B SaaS content strategy.
