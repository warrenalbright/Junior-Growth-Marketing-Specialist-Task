## Article 2: Topical Authority SEO Case Study
- **URL:** https://www.holisticseo.digital/author/koray-tugberk-gubur/
- **Date Published:** Ongoing (updated 2025–2026)
- **Type:** Case Study Collection
- **Author:** Koray Tuğberk GÜBÜR

### Summary
Koray's ongoing collection of SEO case studies documenting real-world
implementations of his Topical Authority and Semantic SEO methodologies
across multiple websites. Each case study includes documented A/B tests,
traffic changes, and entity optimization strategies. His foundational
formula — **Topical Authority = Topical Coverage + Historical Data** —
and his concept of **Cost of Retrieval** ("the easier it is for search
engines to retrieve and interpret your content, the higher its value")
are among the most cited frameworks in modern AI-era SEO. Directly
relevant to AI-powered content production as it provides a data-backed
methodology for structuring content that both humans and AI systems
can efficiently retrieve and understand.
