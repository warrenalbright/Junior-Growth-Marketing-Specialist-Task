- **Type:** Practitioner Review Article (11 min read)

### Summary
James reviews the best AI search monitoring tools in 2026 based on
hands-on testing across virtually every serious platform on the market.
He argues that early AI visibility tools were fundamentally flawed —
pulling data through official APIs that bore no resemblance to real user
experience. The new generation of tools instead visits LLMs directly and
prompts them the way actual customers would. Covers tools including
Profound, Peec AI, and others, evaluating brand mention tracking,
citation monitoring, sentiment analysis, and share of voice across
ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

### Why This Matters
Measurement is a core component of AI-powered SEO content production —
you cannot optimize what you cannot track. James provides a practitioner
verdict on which tools actually work in 2026, drawn from real testing
rather than vendor claims.
