## Article 1: AI Content Wasn't Good Enough. Now It Is.
- **URL:** https://ahrefs.com/blog/ai-content-wasnt-good-enough-now-it-is/
- **Date Published:** March 16, 2026
- **Type:** Blog Article
- **Author:** Ryan Law (Ahrefs)

### Summary
Ryan argues that AI-generated content has crossed a quality threshold where
it is now functionally indistinguishable from human-written content for many
use cases. The piece challenges the SEO community to honestly acknowledge
this shift rather than dismiss it — and to adapt content strategy accordingly.
A critical and honest take from someone building AI content workflows at scale
inside one of the world's leading SEO companies.

## Article 2: The Great Decoupling (or Why Your Clicks Are Down and Impressions Up)
- **URL:** https://ahrefs.com/blog/the-great-decoupling/
- **Date Published:** June 17, 2025
- **Type:** Blog Article
- **Author:** Ryan Law (Ahrefs)

### Summary
Ryan explains the "Great Decoupling" phenomenon — where Google Search
impressions continue to rise while organic clicks decline. The core driver
is AI Overviews and zero-click search behavior, where users get answers
directly from the SERP without visiting the source page. This has major
implications for how B2B SaaS companies measure content ROI and structure
their AI-powered SEO content production strategy.

### Why This Matters
Directly relevant to AI-powered SEO content production — any practitioner
building an AI content strategy needs to understand that the traditional
traffic-as-success metric is being disrupted. Ryan provides a clear
framework for adapting measurement and content goals in this new reality.
