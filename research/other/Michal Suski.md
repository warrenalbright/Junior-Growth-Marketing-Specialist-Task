## Article 1: Scraped AI Answers vs. API Results from LLMs - Is There a Difference? [AI Search Study]
- **URL:** https://surferseo.com/blog/llm-scraped-ai-answers-vs-api-results/
- **Date Published:** December 5, 2025 (Updated February 3, 2026)
- **Type:** Original Research Study
- **Author:** Jakub Sadowski (contributor: Michał Suski)

### Summary
Michał initiated this study after being challenged at the Chiang Mai SEO
conference by Felix Norton (Woww) to quantify the real difference between
scraping AI results and using official LLM APIs. He commissioned Surfer's
data science team to test 1,000 prompts and compare results. Key finding:
there is a significant difference between actual AI answers and what their
APIs return — which has major implications for how AI visibility tools
collect and report data, and how practitioners should interpret AI SEO
metrics.

### Why This Matters
Any AI-powered SEO content production strategy depends on accurate
measurement of AI visibility. This study directly challenges the reliability
of API-based tracking tools and provides data-backed evidence for why
scraping actual LLM responses produces more accurate results.

## Article 2: SEO Isn't Dead - Michał Suski on AI, Surfer & the Future of Search
- **URL:** https://www.fortismedia.com/en/articles/michal-suski-surferseo-interview/
- **Date Published:** March 18, 2026
- **Type:** Interview Article (6 min read)
- **Author:** Jorge Costa (interviewing Michał Suski)

### Summary
Michał shares his perspective on AI-driven search evolution and how SEO
agencies can stay competitive in 2026. He argues that SEO is not dead but
fundamentally transforming — with AI search requiring a different approach
to content optimization, authority building, and visibility measurement.
As co-founder of Surfer SEO, his insights are grounded in product
development and thousands of customer use cases across B2B SaaS companies.

### Why This Matters
A practitioner-level view from someone who builds the tools the industry
uses — giving unique insight into where AI-powered SEO content production
is heading from both a strategic and technical perspective.
